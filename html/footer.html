This application is part of my thesis research concerning the evaluation of explosion consequences in relation to distance for various chemical substances (AN & H₂). Its purpose is purely academic, intended as a preliminary assessment for establishing safe distances. I sincerely welcome any constructive feedback and criticism to enrich this study, both from the perspective of chemical engineering and the implementation of JavaScript technology.
